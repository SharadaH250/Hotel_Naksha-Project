ABOUT

The Naksha Gangavathi is centrally located in Gangavathi city, adjacent to an 18-hole golf course. It operates 150 rooms and suites and offers over 40,000 square feet of conference and banqueting space for both indoor and outdoor events. The Naksha Gangavathi also features fitness facilities which include a lawn tennis court.

SERVICES

For a relaxed dining experience within the comfort of your room, the 24-hour In-room dining service with a multi-cuisine.
The 184 beautifully appointed suites and rooms at Naksha Gangavathi have been elegantly designed and exquisitely furnished. The rooms overlook either the sprawling golf course or the hotel’s inviting azure pool.
Exuding sheer luxury, the 2456 square feet Ballroom opens onto a pre-function area. Our chefs craft gourmet banquet menus to suit corporate events, product launches, conferences, seminars, private parties, weddings and more.
